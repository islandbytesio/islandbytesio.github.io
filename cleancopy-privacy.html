Clean Copy · IslandBytes

Privacy Policy

Clean Copy does not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal data.

How It Works

Clean Copy handles selected text and table content within the browser to convert it into formats such as CSV, JSON, TSV, or SQL. No webpage content, browsing history, or personal details are transmitted externally.

Local Processing

All core functionality runs locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your device as part of the extension's operation.

Payments

Transactions and subscriptions are handled securely by a third-party payment processor, ExtensionPay. Clean Copy itself does not retain or process any payment details.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at support@islandbytes.io.
